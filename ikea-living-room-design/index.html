Living & Dining — Design Plan

A comprehensive, circulation-first plan for a long pass-through living/dining room that connects the foyer, kitchen, balcony and two bedrooms — built on existing furniture, completed with IKEA-only additions.

1 · The room

Theo's 2 BHK floor plan. Our room is #2 LIV/DIN (10'0" × 23'5½") — the central spine of the home. We design to the measured 296 × 706 cm, not the rounded layout figure.

2 · Layout & circulation

Kitchen + balcony cluster at the top, foyer at the bottom, and the two bedroom doors face off across the exact middle — so dining goes top, living goes bottom, and the middle stays a clear ~110 cm crossroads.

  • Dining pulled to the top — serves the kitchen, keeps balcony light.
  • Living anchored at the foyer end.
  • Bedroom-door crossroads kept furniture-free.
  • 240 cm BESTÅ media wall fits inside 296 cm with reveals.
  • Low KALLAX divides zones, keeps sightlines open.
  • Two rugs zone the open plan.
